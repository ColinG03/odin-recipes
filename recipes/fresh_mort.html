FRESH MORT

Description

The FRESH MORT sando is like the grace of god blessing your tastebuds with each bite.

Try it warm with fried MORT and melted provolone, or enjoy it the traditional way with will cold MORT straight outta the fridge.

Ingredients

  • Focaccia
  • Tomato
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • MORT
  • Provolone
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Hot Sauce (Optional)

Steps

  1. Fry mort in pan(only if you are going with warm option). near the end, after you've flipped the mort, add the provolone on top so it starts melting.
  2. Cut and toast focaccia.
  3. Wash and slice tomato and lettuce.
  4. Mustard and mayo on the focaccia and assemble, adding salt and pepper on top of the tomatoes (sticks best there)
  5. Enjoy!
