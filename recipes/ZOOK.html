ZOOK

Description

We're putting an end to dookie zookie. Sink your teeth into this succelent, zesty and delicious ZOOK!

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Lemons
  • Lemon and Herbs Spice Blend
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Thyme
  • S&P500

Steps

  1. Slice ZOOK lengthwise, not too thin, not too thick.
  2. Cut lemons and juice over the ZOOK in a big bowl, then add white wine vinegar and spices.
  3. Grill ZOOK on BBQ for about 5 mins each side medium heat.
  4. Enjoy!
